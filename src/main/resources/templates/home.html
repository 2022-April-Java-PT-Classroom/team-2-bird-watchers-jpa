What is Bird Watching?

  • Bird watching is in its most basic form is observing any activity wherein birds are envolved in for recreaction or citizen science.
  • Many watch for recreation or because of their fascination of the species.
  • Others do it for science and record their behavior.
  • You can watch birds with the naked eye, some prefer to use binoculars or telescopes.
